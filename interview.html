Blown to Bits

Hal Abelson, Ken Ledeen, Harry Lewis

The Global Position System (GPS) allows you to be located anywhere on the Earth in three different dimensions. This is done with the assistance of 24 satellites that are over 12,000 miles above the Earth. Phones have similar tracking systems, but they aren't as precise.

Installation of Event Data Recorders in cars has become very popular in recent years. They are used as valid evidence in court to prove innocence and are insightful when investigating a car accident. They record data on speed, braking time, turn signal status, and seat belts.

Small, seemingly useless fragments of information often connect to a bigger picture or conspiracy. This is a useful tool when analyzing crime.
